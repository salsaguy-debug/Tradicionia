4. Core Natural Language Intent Rules

The routing engine automatically parses performer inputs to fetch relevant database segments. These are the main routing rules:

  • Rule A (Payments & Balances): Triggered by keywords like pay, money, check, stipend, paid, balance, pago, dinero. Indexes the Payments ledger matching your Performer ID.
  • Rule B/F (Costumes & Inventory): Triggered by keywords like gear, inventory, costume, conga, shoe, uniform, dress, vestuario, ropa. Accesses Inventory, Attendance, and Tradicion_Org.
  • Rule G (Profiles & Contacts): Triggered by keywords like birthday, birth, profile, contact, email, phone, active, buddy, manager. Restores buddy and contact channels.

5. Suggested Actions & Quick Queries Dropdown

For seamless administration and hand-free operations, the empty chat view displays a premium, glassmorphic dropdown list containing five quick queries. Selecting any option instantly populates the chat workspace and executes the lookup. Below is the operational detail for each available query:

👔 Assigned Inventory

Dispatches: "What costumes are checked out to me?" / "¿Qué trajes tengo asignados?"

Indexes the Inventory database matching your Performer ID. Returns your checked-out dance costumes, instruments (such as congas), checkout dates, and physical condition.

📅 Attendance Status

Dispatches: "What is my rehearsal attendance history?" / "Cual es mi asistencia en los ensayos?"

Deep-scans the Attendance sheet. Summarizes your rehearsal logs, calculating total attended rehearsals, excused absences, unexcused absences, and your overall attendance percentage.

📖 Organizational Manual

Dispatches: "Who is my buddy and direct manager?" / "¿Quién es mi buddy y asignado?"

Queries the Tradicion_Org curriculum level and hierarchy records. Returns your assigned administrative coordinator and choreography buddy pairing details.

🎖️ Request Report Card

Dispatches: "Please show my Performer Report Card." / "Por favor, muestra mi Reporte de Progreso de Performer."

Connects to the Tradición Performer Report Cards tab. Extracts real-time progress ratings, compliance, syllabus tasks completed, and direct feedback notes from the Director.

📅 90 Day Event Summary

Dispatches: "Please show my 90 Day Event Summary schedule." / "Por favor, muestra mi Resumen de Eventos de 90 Días."

Syncs with Live Performance Google Calendars. Compiles all upcoming performances, checking invitations, RSVP statuses, dates, and locations.

6. Input Actions Tray Details

At the bottom of the chat viewport, the following utility elements are available:

  • Copy Thread (content_copy): Formats the entire session into a clean markdown document and copies it to your clipboard.
  • Download Log (file_download): Exporter to download the active conversation history as a .txt file.
  • Neon Microphone (mic): Audio voice capture shortcut to dictate queries hands-free.

7. Profile Updates & Cash Payment Logging

Performer details can be modified securely from the platform, and cash contributions can be logged directly into the external ledger:

  • Update Modal: Access from the bottom of the left sidebar. dispatches double-lock authentication tokens before writing.
  • Medical Uploads: Decodes Base64 medical document blobs, uploads them securely to Google Drive under a dedicated Health Certificates folder, makes the link viewable, and registers the entry in the Health_certificates ledger.

Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) — Tradición Dance Company

1. Purpose & Scope

This SOP ensures professional integrity, accurate rehearsal planning, fair financial tracking, and full transparency for all members of Tradición Dance Company and Salsa Guy Richmond, LLC. It outlines the expectations for member conduct, financial accountability, performance standards, and administrative dispute resolution.

2. Attendance & Rehearsal Protocols

2.1 Calendar Management (Mandatory)

Members must manage their RSVP status on all Google Calendar invites sent to their registered email address. Status must be updated as soon as a conflict is known.

  • "No": The only status that excuses a member from rehearsal and waives the associated fee.
  • "Maybe": Considered a "hold." If the member does not attend, a fee is charged.
  • "Awaiting" (No Response): Treated as a "Yes." Failure to show results in an automated fee.
2.2 Rehearsal Check-In
  • Physical Sign-In: Members must sign the physical attendance sheet upon arrival.
  • Data Verification: The email used on the sign-in sheet must match the Google Calendar invite email to prevent syncing errors.
2.3 Rehearsal Conduct
  • Limit cell phone use and avoid inappropriate language.
  • Treat all coordinators and property with respect.
  • Uniform: Only Tradición T-shirts may be worn to practice.
  • Substance abuse or intentional injury to others will result in immediate expulsion.

3. Financial Accountability

Participation is voluntary with no guaranteed monetary compensation. Benefits include free lessons, discounts, use of professional costumes, two performer T-shirts, and occasional expense stipends.

3.1 Dues & Penalties Structure
Category Amount Description / Terms
Monthly Dues $15.00 Due the first Monday of every month (no later than the following Saturday).
Late Fees $5.00 Applied weekly to outstanding balances. Compounds over multiple months (e.g., 1 week late = $20 total; 2 weeks late = $25 total).
RSVP / Absence Fees $5.00 Applied automatically for unexcused absences (RSVP marked as Yes, Maybe, or Awaiting) or marking "No" but attending anyway.
3.2 Payments & Eligibility
  • Accepted Methods: Check (made out to Salsa Guy Richmond, LLC), Venmo, or Cash App. PayPal and Zelle are not accepted.
  • Performance Eligibility: Balances exceeding $30.00 will restrict members from participating in upcoming showcases or festivals until settled.

Technical Apps Script Installation & Deploy Details

Follow these steps to deploy this system within your Google Suite environment:

  1. Open your Tradición Master Source Spreadsheet in your web browser.
  2. Navigate to Extensions > Apps Script.
  3. Remove any default placeholder code and create the following project files:
    • Create a script file named Code.gs and paste the backend Apps Script code.
    • Create an HTML file named Index.html and paste the SPA dashboard structure.
    • Create an HTML file named Styles.html and paste the custom CSS.
    • Create an HTML file named JavaScript.html and paste the controller script.
  4. In the Apps Script editor, click the gear icon for Project Settings. Under Script Properties, add a property named GEMINI_API_KEY with your Google Gemini developer key.
  5. Click Deploy > New Deployment. Choose Web App:
    • Execute as: Me (Your Google Account).
    • Who has access: Anyone (Ensures correct handshake validation without enforcing complex organization limits).
  6. Copy the resulting Web App URL and paste it into the Network Panel in the dashboard settings to bind communication channels. You are ready to go!
